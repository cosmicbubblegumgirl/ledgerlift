Case study

From spreadsheet anxiety to decision-ready cash foresight.

LedgerLift is designed for small teams that need to know whether they can hire, buy stock, pay suppliers, or wait out a late invoice without guessing.

Problem

Profit was visible. Cash timing was not.

Owners often know revenue is coming, but not whether it arrives before payroll, rent, taxes, or supplier commitments.

UX move

Put the decision inside the forecast.

The interface starts with cash runway, then lets users test the actual business choices that change the next 45 days.

Outcome

Clean handoff to the next person.

Editable documents, notes, and database rows let a bookkeeper or advisor pick up the context without rebuilding it.
